## Leaving Ravenloft

After finding Argynvosts skull in the crypt beneath Castle Ravenloft, the party forges a plan: the next morning Yaroslav will carry the skull with help from Thaddeus and run as fast as he can towards Vallaki, hoping to escape the castle before anyone can spoil this ingenious plan. As part of their preparations, Reese uses his magical abilities to request aid from both Ezmeralda and the Martikov family in Vallaki to get as safely as possible past Vallaki. Ezmeralda replies that she will leave Ireena to the previously returned van Richten, who wants to lure in Strahd using her as bait.

Come the next morning, Yaroslav and Thaddeus set the plan in motion, while all - apart from Yaroslav - board the carriage to drive them towards Vallaki. Yaroslav manages to sprint out of the castle with the shrunken dragon skull wrapped under his arm, overtaking the carriage and bolting over the rickety drawbridge. Without anything really happening (certainly because of the astonishing quality of the plan), the party finds both a conspiracy of ravens and Ezmeralda on their way to Vallaki: the manifesting Martikov guide the group around Vallaki and distract the guards, while Ezmeralda lends her wagon to transport the large skull.

On a crossing after Vallaki, Ezmeralds departs to the tower to check on van Richten and Ireena to prevent any major mishaps brought forth by her aging mentor. The party continues with her wagon towards Argynvostholt.

## The Crypt

Although Ezmeraldas draft horse refuses to walk the last yards towards the decrepit keep, the party ends up breaking into the graveyard through the iron fence, carrying the skull on their shoulders. Ilizeiros continues to dispell the magic barrier blocking entry to the crypt. After the skull is lowered unto its proper resting place, a magical light appears and slowly grows in intensity. Before long however, Strahd appears with Anastrasya in tow and is unamused with the grand theft skull and demands it back. After he casts some form of powerful magic to summon the dead from the graveyard above, he appears to leave the scene, leaving the cleanup to his undead minions and Anastrasya.
