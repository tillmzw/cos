# The Dinner

The party has dinner with Lord Strahd. 

The party gets food served that they like.

In the middle of the dinner Strahd surprises the party with the somehow animated body of their former companion Benjamin.

He accuses the party of abandoning Benjamin and also of the murder of innocent werewolves.

## The Skulls

The party uses Benjamin to get to the skull of the dragon Argynvost and plans a heist to get it out of the castle.
