The voice calls to Ilizeiros and helps him interrogating Lady Wachter. She made a deal with Anastrasya.

Lady Wachter is executed and her minions branded. The baron takes over Vallaki once more.

The party debates where to go and decides to go to the amber crypt. Ilizeiros has withdrawal symptoms that are getting worse.

On the way to the crypt, the party notices that they are being followed. Werewolves! At the edge of the woods the party is surrounded by the werewolf pack that they encountered in the woods around Krezk. Their leader is with them. The party tries to intimidate them but it only works on their lieutenants. A fight ensues...
