The long dead ruler of {{% mdref argynvostholt Argynvostholt %}}. He lead a group of noble knights - the Order of the Silver Dragon - who opposed {{% mdref strahd-of-zarovich Strahd %}} and his armies. He was secretly a (silver) dragon. Once Strahd defeated him and his order, Strahd took the skull of Argynvost with him as a trophy to his {{% mdref castle-ravenloft Castle %}}
